Privacy Policy — Cricket Scoring

Last updated: 3 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Cricket Scoring mobile application ("the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. No account required

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identity information.

2. Information the App handles

  • Match data you enter: team names, player names, scores, overs, tournaments, and similar cricket scoring information. This is stored locally on your device by default.
  • Anonymous device identifier: the App generates a random identifier (a UUID) the first time it runs. It is not linked to your real identity and is used only to group your own backups together if you choose to use cloud sync.

3. Optional cloud backup (sync)

The App works fully offline. If you choose to use the "Sync" feature, your match and tournament data and your anonymous device identifier are sent to our backend server and stored in a database so the data can be backed up and restored. We do not sell this data or share it with third parties for advertising.

4. What we do NOT collect

  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or contacts.
  • No precise or background location.
  • No advertising identifiers; the App contains no ads and no third-party analytics or tracking.

5. Data retention & deletion

Data stored on your device is removed when you delete a match or uninstall the App. To request deletion of any data backed up to our server, contact us at the email below with your device identifier (shown in the App's Settings screen) and we will delete the associated records.

6. Children

The App is a general-audience sports utility and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
