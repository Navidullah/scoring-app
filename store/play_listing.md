## Full description (max 4000 chars)

CricLive is the fast, clean way to score your cricket matches ball by ball — and let your friends and family follow along LIVE from anywhere, even in their browser. Whether it's a gully match, a tennis-ball tournament, a club game or a friendly, CricLive keeps the scorebook in your pocket and works fully offline.

★ LIVE MATCH FOLLOWING
Score a match and share a single link — anyone can watch the live scoreboard update in real time, in any browser, no app needed. Browse all live matches inside the app and follow ball-by-ball.

★ FAST BALL-BY-BALL SCORING
Tap to score runs, extras (wide, no-ball, bye, leg-bye), wickets and free hits. Automatic strike rotation, run rate, required run rate and target tracking. Made one mistake? Undo instantly.

★ BUILT FOR EVERY KIND OF CRICKET
• Leather ball or tennis/tape-ball — your choice
• Turn LBW off for tennis-ball rules
• Free hit after a no-ball
• Run-out: pick which batter is out
• Retire a batter and bring them back

★ FULL SCORECARDS & STATS
Complete batting and bowling figures, strike rates, economy, fall of wickets, Manhattan chart, and an automatic Player of the Match. Share any scorecard as a clean image or a printable PDF in one tap.

★ TOURNAMENTS
Create round-robin or knockout tournaments, auto-generate fixtures, and track the points table with Net Run Rate and the championship bracket.

★ LEADERBOARDS
See the top run-scorers and wicket-takers across all your matches.

★ WORKS OFFLINE, SYNCS TO THE CLOUD
No internet at the ground? No problem. Everything is saved on your device and syncs to the cloud when you're back online — your matches are never lost.

★ NO SIGN-UP, NO CLUTTER
Start scoring in seconds. No account, no ads getting in the way — just a beautiful, modern scorer with a dark and light theme.

Perfect for players, captains, scorers, club organisers and cricket lovers across India, Pakistan, Bangladesh and beyond.

Download CricLive and turn every match into a proper match — scored, shared and remembered.
